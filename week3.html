Week 3 Updates

This was a kick-ass week for us!     • Outreach - Bangalore Bus & Metro transport officials

      We have contacted both bus and metro rail officials, and have a meeting scheduled with both of them to discuss our proposal further!

      We have integrated with ICICI test APIs for QR based payment on buses and UPI payments for Autorickshaws (with the 'Mandate' feature of committing to a payment in the future, to ensure trust of service between users and drivers, similar to smart contracts on the blockchain).
