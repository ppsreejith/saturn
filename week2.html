(😈 is in the) Details

  • PR

    • Outreach - NEC

      We have an agreement for a pilot project in Ahmedabad, India. We are integrating with their APIs to obtain bus routing and ETA details. We also received aggregate historical ticketing information, from which we plan to predict occupancy until the deep learning module is actually deployed.

    • Outreach - ICICI Bank

      We are in talks with the payments team of ICICI bank, to use their infrastructure for UPI payments. We are presenting them a pitch in the first week of May, to be Saturn's exclusive payments partner



  • Product

    • Customer /Driver App

      We refined some glitches in the apps and have the end to end booking flow UI working

    • Deep Learning for Occupancy (On the Server)

      We implemented the YOLO v3 paper in pytorch, and trained it on a subset of the COCO dataset. We've deployed it on the server, and have wired it up to a mode in the driver app. The model has 94% acccuracy in identifying people in the frame, and their location in the image. It has ~99.6% accuracy in predicting class of occcupancy ({empty, few people, crowded, heavily crowded} classes)

    • Server

      Began implementing the Graphhopper routing algorithm for most optimal bus route identification

    • V0 Government Dashboard

      We built the V0 of the government dashboard - comprising of heatmaps and route efficiency maps
